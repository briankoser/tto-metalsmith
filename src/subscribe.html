Subscribe

The easiest way to listen to a podcast is to subscribe. A good podcast app will automatically download and alert you when a new episode is available. Click on your platform to find out how:

Android

  1. Download Podcast Addict, a free podcast app.
  2. Tap the Add new Podcast button.
  3. Tap the Search Engine tile.
  4. Search for Ten to One.
  5. Tap the Subscribe button.
  6. Tap the menu icon. Then tap Update, Automatic refresh, Enable Podcasts automatic refresh.
  7. Tap the back button to go back to Settings. Then tap Download, Automatic download. Now, whenever we release a new episode, it will automatically download to your phone.
  8. You will get a notification when an episodes has downloaded. Tap the notification to see the new episode. Or, open the app and tap on the Ten to One tile.
  9. Tap the Play button to play the episode. If the episode has not downloaded, a Download button will be in place of the Play button. Tap Download to download the episode.
  10. Enjoy the podcast!

Windows

  1. Download and install iTunes.
  2. Open iTunes and click File, then Subscribe to Podcast.
  3. Enter our URL: http://tto.koser.us/feed.rss and click OK.
  4. You do not need to sync, but you should select Automatically download new episodes for your subscriptions, then click Continue.
  5. Listen to an episode by double-clicking on it.
  6. To listen to our new episode every two weeks, just open iTunes. Any new episodes will be automatically downloaded.
  7. Enjoy the podcast!
